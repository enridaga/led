The cadenza at the end of her opening air &mdash; the whole of which was listened to with a stillness quite singular &mdash; called down a hurricane of applause. Voice, style, execution, manner, acting &mdash; all delighted. The triumph was achieved. </span></p>

Those were the exclamations that fell from the lips of Her Majesty, whom I had never before seen thus moved to enthusiasm.</span></p>

As for the file of carriages, it seemed as interminable as it was dense. </span></p>
