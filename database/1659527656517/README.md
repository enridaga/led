<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">They made me their secretary; and my head went to work to make the music of the Choral Society worth hearing in old cathedralled Lincoln.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">I planned, I visited, I wooed, I entreated, till I obtained the aid and co-operation of the best musicians and the best singers in the ancient city.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>Like every true reformer, I had to put down the authority of the imperfect, and put the authoritative perfect in its place.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>Over the company of raw amateurs</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri;">―</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">despite some grumbling</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri;">―</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">I succeeded in placing the most perfect &ldquo;singer at sight,&rdquo; and most thoroughly experienced person in the music of Handle, to be found in the whole city, as conductor; the best violinist in the city, as leader; the best alto and tenor singers in the city, as leaders of their parts in the choruses, and as principal solo singers; the best violoncello player in Lincoln, as leader on his instrument; while I also secured the aid of an experienced trumpeter.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>We already had the aid of a good double-bass player, who was also a sound timist.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>[&hellip;]</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">[Cooper successfully sought patronage for the society&rsquo;s concerts] and the incumbent of the most central church in the city granted us the use of it for our public concerts of sacred music.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>Mr. Whall, the most thoroughly competent organist in Lincoln, presided at the organ; and, before a crowded audience, the transcendent &ldquo;Messiah,&rdquo; the noble &ldquo;Dettingen Te Deum,&rdquo; the brilliant and warlike &ldquo;Judas Maccabaeus,&rdquo; the gorgeous &ldquo;Solomon,&rdquo; the sublime &ldquo;Israel in Egypt,&rdquo; and other oratorios of Handel were performed with an enthusiasm that had never before been witnessed in Lincoln.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>The &ldquo;Creation&rdquo; of Haydn, and scattered choral pieces of Mozart and Beethoven, were also given.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">Nor was the solo singing of a mean character.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>Our conductor, George Brooke, of the cathedral choir, would have attracted admiration, as a bass singer of great original powers of expression, and great capability of execution, with the most critical audience in the kingdom.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>The tenor singing of dear departed Charles Ashton</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri;">―</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">a universal favourite in Lincoln</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri;">―</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">was the sweetest I ever heard, except Braham&rsquo;s.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>Mr. Knowles, our alto solo singer, was not only a very pleasing vocalist, but a competent musician; and is, at the moment I write, a member of the choir in St. George&rsquo;s Chapel, Windsor.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">[&hellip;]</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">What mad enthusiasm I felt for music!<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>I often sat up the greater part of a night to transact the writing necessary for the furtherance of the prosperity of that Choral Society.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>[&hellip;]<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>The enjoyment</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri;">―</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">the rapture</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: Calibri;">―</span><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">I had in listening to the music, was more than a reward for whatever time I gave to the society, or interest I sacrificed for it.</span></p>
