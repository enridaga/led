<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">[The prisoner, Susannah Kirby, was acquitted of pocketpicking on 15 May 1755]</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Margaret Nelson. I was at the rehearsal at&nbsp;St. Paul's walking with my arm in Mr. Law's. I felt a hand in my pocket; and said to him, there is a hand in my pocket; I bid him not say any thing till I saw whether it was picked or not[.]</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Q. When was this?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">M. Nelson. It was on the 15th of April.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Q. In the church, or church-yard?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">M. Nelson. In the church. It was after the music was over; there was a very great crowd of people walking. I told Mr. Law my purse was gone, and he went and laid hold of the prisoner.[...]</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Q. How long before this happened had you felt your purse in your pocket?</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">M. Nelson.&nbsp;</span><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">I had taken my purse out about half an hour before, to give to the collection at the door, and put it in my pocket again.</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">Q. Are you sure you put it in your pocket?</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">&nbsp;</span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-family: Arial, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 14.6667px;">M.
