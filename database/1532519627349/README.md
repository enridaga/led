<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">[M]y thoughts turned to November the fifth, Bonfire night. Practically all of the villagers turned out to witness the Celebrations [&hellip;] Stephen Gillham&mdash;Fred&rsquo;s father--&nbsp;had been having the band practising for weeks, all of its members wore costumes, Levi Cheal&mdash;Frank&rsquo;s father&mdash;played the big bass instrument which hung round his body, he was dressed as a Scotchman with kilt and being six feet tall made a fine looking figure. The torch light procession, headed by the band paraded round about the village calling at the big houses to play a few selections, not forgetting Elliott&rsquo;s shop [the family business], at each stop repeating the old Rhyme: </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Remember remember the fifth of November the gunpowder treason and plot </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">I know no reason why gunpowder treason&nbsp;should ever be forgot </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Guy Fawkes guy 'twas his intent to blow up the King and Parliment&nbsp;<em>[sic]&nbsp;</em></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Four score barrels of powder below to prove old Englands overthrow </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">By Gods providence he got catched with a dark lantern and a light match </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Holler boys holler make the bells ring burn the Pope and God save the King </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">Hip, hip, hip, hooray.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';">&nbsp;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: 'Times New Roman';"><span style="font-size: 11pt;">When the procession returned, its numbers doubled, the band played its final items in front of the Wheatsheaf then the Rhyme was repeated for the last time.&nbsp;</span></p>
