<p style="margin: 0px; font-size: 9.1px; font-family: Arial;">Was taken to a performance of Mozart conducted by Herbert Von Karajan. I remember he had something wrong with his legs and was almost carried to the podium where he then stood unaided for the whole symphony&hellip; it was magical, it was my first introduction to a full live symphony orchestra performance and I have been hooked ever since.</p>
