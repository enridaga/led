<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Mary Foster: On the 17th of August last, between 6 and 7 o'clock in the morning, I was on the marsh wall, by the mill, at&nbsp;Poplar, walking along I was alone.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">[&hellip;]</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">There are no houses, it is an open field. I had two calicoe curtains in my hand belonging to Mr. Crush; the prisoner [Jer. Main] met me and stopped me.[&hellip;]&nbsp;&nbsp;He took me in his arms, and carried me into the rushes, betwixt the river Thames and the bank, without saying another&nbsp;word to me; there he used me in a very indecent manner [and stole my belongings].&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">[&hellip;]</span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Q. from the prisoner: Did I not ask you to go in at the first publick house to drink? [after the alleged attack]</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Foster. <p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0.0001pt; font-size: medium; font-family: Calibri, sans-serif;"><span style="font-size: 11pt; font-family: Arial, sans-serif;">Q. How long is the street?</span></p>
