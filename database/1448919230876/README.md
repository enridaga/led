<p>Hitler has invaded Austria: that is at 10 last night his army crossed the frontier, unresisted. The Austrian national anthem was heard on the wireless for the last time.&nbsp; We got a snatch of dance music from Vienna.&nbsp; This fact, which combines with the Russian trials, like drops of dirty water mixing, puts its thorn into my morning:a pernickety one spent over notes.</p>
