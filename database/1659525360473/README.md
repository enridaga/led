<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">[T]he earnestness of my prayers for holiness soon raised a flame around me.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>Others began to pray for holiness.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span>And then, in company with a few earnest young men, I began to meet once a week in the house of a female class-leader who for many years had been noted for fervid devotion.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="mso-ascii-font-family: Calibri; mso-fareast-font-family: Calibri; mso-hansi-font-family: Calibri; mso-bidi-font-family: 'Times New Roman';">I read Bramwell [James Sigston&rsquo;s <em>Life of William Bramwell</em>] on my knees by three in the morning.<span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;
