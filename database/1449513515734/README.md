<p style="margin: 0px; font-size: 9.1px; font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;- of a yong unknown (here) Swedish conductor who, in the second half, conducted a glorious performance of Sibelius' second symphony. Was it Paavo Berglund? Well before his residence at Bournemouth.2. - of the night of the peasouper in the mid fifties, when the air-conditioning in the hall couldn't cope, and a mist formed in the concert hall itself! Going back over Hungerford bridge, walking in the middle, I could not see the railings either side!</p>
