To repeat his own words, addressed to myself: &ldquo;You have so overwhelmed me with kindness in this country that I shall be quite sorry when the time comes for me to leave you.&rdquo; The programme comprised his &ldquo;Angelus&rdquo; for strings, a chorus for female voices, a pianoforte piece, and some songs; and finally, amid a scene of great excitement, he himself played the finale of Schubert's &ldquo;Divertissement a la Hongroise&rdquo; and his own Hungarian rhapsody in A minor.
