<p>The week after the races Mrs. Crouch took her benefit, a great house ; Mrs. Crouch played Clara, and I Carlos, with Comus ; our duet, "Oh, thou wert born to please me," was wonderfully well received. On Wednesday, the 29th, we commenced a four nights' engagement at Leeds, in the " Maid of the Mill ;" Patty, Mrs. Crouch ; Lord Aim worth by myself. " Love in a Village," " The Duenna," and " Lionel and Clarissa, to excellent houses.</p>
