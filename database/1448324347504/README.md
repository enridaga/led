<p>I then went to see the cellist Rosenstein to play through the&nbsp;<em>Ballade&nbsp;</em>for Nurok's concert. I had played with him several times before, four years ago at the 'Evenings of Contemporary Music', and I was not expecting it to be anything like as good as with Belo&uacute;sov, but in fact Rosenstein showed a lot of temperament, tone and liveliness: it was a very decent performance and his&nbsp;<em>pizzicato&nbsp;</em>was even faster than Belo&uacute;sov's.&nbsp;</p>
