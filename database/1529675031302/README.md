<p class="MsoNormal">The road here is beautiful, particularly about la Cava. I walked up to the Convent of the Trinit&agrave;; it stands on the brink of a deep ravine in the middle of the hills, which are tossed into a hundred different shapes and covered with foliage&mdash;a magnificent situation. The convent is very large, and well kept; it contains fifty monks, who were most of them walking about the road. Here were all the raw materials requisite for a romance&mdash;a splendid setting sun, mountains, convent, flock of goats, evening bell, friars, and peasants.</p>
