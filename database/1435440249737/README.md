<p>At Mr. Wessel's German soir&eacute;e I heard some songs of Shobert, a new author. His 'Erl King,' sung by Madame Schroeder, and accompanied on the piano-forte by Madame Dulcken, certainly was a most terrific thing of its kind. The alarming intonation of the vocalist, and the awful thunder which the pianist threw into the bass, had a dramatic effect purely German.</p>
