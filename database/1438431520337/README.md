### language


*urn:iso:std:iso:639:ed-3:eng*

### value


<p>The Yurasovsky residence is an imposing apartment; his parents are clearly well off. He is very envious of my success with Jurgenson (200 roubles for the Sonata). He played me his embryonic piano concerto - not worth any attention - and then we went to the Koussevitzky concert. It was a lovely programme: Rachmaninov's&nbsp;<em>Isle of the Dead&nbsp;</em>and Second Piano Concerto, and Scriabin's&nbsp;<em>Extase</em>. I adored the last named, especially the place where the high moaning of the woodwinds is doubled by the horns down below, a marvellous passage.</p>
