<p class="MsoNormal" style="mso-pagination: none; mso-layout-grid-align: none; text-autospace: none;"><span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 13.0pt; font-family: Arial; mso-bidi-font-family: Monaco; color: #262626; mso-ansi-language: EN-US;">I despair of giving an idea of the devoutness of the audience [at a Good Friday service].
